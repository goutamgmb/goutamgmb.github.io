I am a second year PhD student at the Computer Vision Lab in ETH Zurich, under the supervision of Prof. Luc Van Gool. I am also advised by Dr. Martin Danelljan and Dr. Radu Timofte. My main research interests are Computer Vision and its applications, especially in the tasks of visual object tracking and segmentation.

I received my M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Linköping University, Sweden in 2019. During this time, I also worked as a research assistant at the Computer Vision Lab, Linköping University. I completed my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering at IIT Bombay, India in 2016.
